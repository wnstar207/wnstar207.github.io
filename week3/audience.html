Target Audience

Description

The target audience is residents of and visitors to Fish Haven, Preston, and Soda Springs, ID who need to know what the weather will be. Primary users will likely be adults of any age, and older teens.


Personas

Katie

Age: 37
Occupation: SAHM, children attending local elementary and high schools
Education: College Graduate
Platform: Home PC
Special Considerations:
School closures, road conditions


Daniel

Age: 49
Occupation: Truck Driver
Education: High School Graduate
Platform: Smartphone
Special Considerations:
Road conditions, business/truck stop closures, hotels if he gets snowed in


Scenarios

1. How hot will it be today?
2. Should my kids wear jackets or shorts to school today?
3. When will the storm get here, and how bad will it be?
4. Do I need snow tires/chains today?
5. What roads are closed today?
